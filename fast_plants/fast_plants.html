Make a graph

  1. Make a graph by clicking the Graph button.
  2. Drag the "Day" attribute from the Days table to the horizontal (x) axis and the "Height" attribute to the vertical (y) axis.
  3. Select a row in the Days table:
    1. Where is the data point in the graph?
    2. Click a point in the graph. Where is this data point in the table?
    3. Highlight multiple points in a graph by holding the shift key down on your keyboard and selecting several points or by dragging over an area of the graph to select multiple points. What happens to the table?
  4. Drag the “Flowers” attribute from the Days table into the center of the graph. What happens?
  5. What story does the data tell you?

Intermediate

  • Save your graph by clicking the "Save" link in the top left menu (the three horizontal bars to the left of “Untitled Document”).
  • Create an account on the Concord Cloud. Sign in [or Register] via the CODAP Portal. Now you can save!
  • Resize the graph by option-clicking on the grid. Rescale the graph by clicking the top right menu item (the one with four arrows!).
  • Use the ruler icon in the right-hand sidebar of the graph to plot connecting lines.
  • Make as many graphs as you want to continue exploring the data!

    • Advanced

    1. Create a new attribute in the table by clicking on the ruler in the right-hand sidebar of the table. Use the formula editor to create a formula, or enter the data in manually using the table.
    2. Need help? Click on the ? icon in the top right corner.
