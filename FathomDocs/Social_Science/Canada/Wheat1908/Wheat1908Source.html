This information was extracted from CANSIM Table 001-0010 on Statistics Canada's website, at http://www5.statcan.gc.ca on November 24, 2004, accessed September 2, 2015. Statistics Canada's CANSIM is a multidimensional database containing time series data from over 250 different surveys, updated once a year. As of July 2004 it contains 25 million time series regrouped in over 2,000 tables. Visit their website for further information on the supports provided.
