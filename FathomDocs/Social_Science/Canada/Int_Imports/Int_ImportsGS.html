Getting Started

This case table has information about the kinds of things Canada imports. There are 63 attributes in this table, some explained below, all expressed in millions of Canadian dollars.

Customs based data points are preliminary data, and data on a customs basis are published approximately 50 calendar days after the end of the reference period. Daa on a balance of payments basis are published approximately 50 calendar days after the end of the reference period.

Attribute Key:
  • Year = year the data was taken from
  • Total = total value of imports
  • InedCrude = value of inedible crude product
  • CrudePetroleum = value of crude petroleum products
  • InedFab = value of inedible fabricated materials
  • CoalProducts = value of coal and petroleum products
  • EndProductsIned = value of inedible end products
  • PassAutos = value of passenger auto chassis
  • OtherMotor = value of other motor chassis such as trucks
  • Total_Other_EEC = total value of imports from other countries in the European Economic Community (EEC)
  • Total_Other_OECD = total value of imports from other countries in the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD)
  • Total_Countries = total vale of imports from countries and territories other than the United States, Japan, the EEC, and other OECD countries
