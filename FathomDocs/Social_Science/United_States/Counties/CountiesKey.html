Attribute Key

Here are some attributes explained.

  • CONTY_ID = County ID (as used by TIGER and Census)
  • Precincts = # of polling divisions
  • Reporting = # of polling divisions recorded
  • Bush = # of votes for Bush
  • Kerry = # of votes for Kerry
  • Nader = # of votes for Nader
  • long = longitude in decimal degrees
  • lat = latitude in decimal degrees
  • STATE_FIP = State identifier code
  • CNTY_FIPS = County identifier code
  • FIPS = Concatenation of STATE_FIPS and CNTY_FIPS
  • POP00_SQMI = population 2000 per sq. mile
  • Area_SqMi = Area of county in square miles (divide POP2000 by POP00_SQMI)
  • AREA_SQ_DEG = Area of county polygon using lat/long coordinates
  • PovRate_2002 = % of households living below poverty level in 2002
  • AVE_HH_SZ = avg household size
  • HSEHLD_1_M = 1-person household, male householder
  • HSEHLD_1_F = 1-person household, female householder
  • MARHH_CHD = married-couple family households, w/ own children under 18 yrs
  • MARHH_NO_C = married-couple family households, no own children under 18 yrs
  • MHH_CHILD = other family households, male householder, no wife present, w/ own children under 18 yrs
  • FHH_CHILD = other family households, female householder, no husband present, w/ own children under 18 yrs
  • FAMILIES = # of families
  • AVE_FAM_SZ = avg family size
  • HSE_UNITS = housing units
  • VACANT = vacant housing units
  • OWNER_OCC = owner occupied housing units
  • RENTER_OCC = renter occupied housing units
  • NO_FARMS97 = # of farms,1997
  • AVG_SIZE97 = avg size of farms in 1997
  • CROP_ACR97 = Crop Acreage, 1997
  • AVG_SALE97 = Avg Sales, 1997
