Attribute Key
  • Fatal92 = # of fatalities in 1992
  • BeltPct = % of vehicles with seat belts
  • UnkBeltPct = % of vehicles where it is unknown if they have seat belts
  • BeltEnfType = if the state has primary, secondary, or no laws about seat belt ticketing
