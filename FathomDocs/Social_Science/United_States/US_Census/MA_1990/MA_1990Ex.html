This case table contain information of census data gathered from the IPUMS site at the University of Minnesota. Each case is a person from Massachusetts from either the 1910 census or the 1990 census. There are 1000 of each. You can assume that each was randomly chosen.

Check out the table and graph:

  • Can you find out how old the tenth person in the case table was at the time the census was taken?
  • Click on one of the dots in the graph. What happens in the table?
  • Hover over the highlighted dot in the graph. What information is displayed in the box that appears?

  • Think about it:  What statements can you make based on the data shown? Is there a trend? Are there any relationships?
