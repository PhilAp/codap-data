This case table contains information about the election data from each county in California during the Presidential Election of 2004. We aren't sure why the number of votes for Nader are all 0. Some of the attributes are described below.

  • Totprec = total precincts
  • ReptPrec = precincts reporting
  • pctB (computed) = percentage for Bush
  • PPP (computed) = people per precinct
  • voters = Bush + Kerry + Nader
  • coast = coastal county? 1 = yes, including San Francisco Bay
  • medianIncome = household median income
  • poptotal = total population (not just voters)
  • urban = urban populaton
  • hispanic = those who say they're hispanic or Latino
  • white = those who say they're white only
  • urbFrac (computed) = fraction of the population that's urban
  • whiteFrac (computed) = fraction that's white
  • hispFrac (computed) = fraction that's Hispanic
