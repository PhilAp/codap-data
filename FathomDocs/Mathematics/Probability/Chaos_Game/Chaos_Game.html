Introduction

Chaos Game Simulation

Choose three points in he nit square to be vertices of a triangle.

Start by choosing a point at random in the unit square. For each subsequent "move" one vertex of a triangle is chosen at random and the new point is chosen to be 1/2 the distance from the current point to that vertex.

Animate the t slider to observe the evolution of the game in the graph of x and y.

There are parameters to vary: number of verticies, and ratio of distance being the most obvious. Explore!

There are many online discussions of The Chaos Game. See for example:
