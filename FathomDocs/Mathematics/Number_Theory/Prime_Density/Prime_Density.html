Introduction

You probably know that there are an infinite number of primes, and you may even know a proof of this fact. But how does the spacing of primes change as you go further and further along the number line?

This document is set up to help you investigate this question. The Numbers collection contains the integers from start to stop. Each integer is marked as either prime or not prime.

There are two measures: numPrime counts the number of prime numbers and start just records the starting place.
