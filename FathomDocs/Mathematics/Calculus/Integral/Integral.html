Introduction

In the Data collection, only the Y attribute is free to change. Computed from X and Y are two quantities:

  • Deriv is the change in Y from the previous value to the current one divided by the corresponding change in X.
  • Integral is the total area under the plot of Y versus X from X=1 to the current value of X, plus a constant of integration, C.
