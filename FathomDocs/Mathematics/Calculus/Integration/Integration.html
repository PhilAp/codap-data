Introduction

This document illustrates how to do numerical integration of an arbitrary function. The sliders control the bounds, the granularity, and the constant of integration.

The graphs to the left show the function and its integral as a function of x.

The integral of f(x) from x_lower to x_upper is given in the summary table above.

It is the last value of the attribute cum.
