Introduction

Results from polls taken in 1993, 1994, 2000 and 2003 among students of the Instituto Tecnológico de Costa Rica.

Attributes:

  • ID: student ID number. The first two (before 2000) or four (starting in 2000) digits indicate the year the student was admitted; the other five are (apparently) random but unique within each year.
  • Province: province of origin (Costa Rica has seven provinces; ITCR is in Cartago). "Other" refers to foreign students. The polls in 1993 and 1994 didn't register this attribute.
  • Major: program the student is enrolled in
  • Sex: of the student..
  • Height: height (as reported by the student), in centimeters
  • Weight: weight (as reported by the student), in kilograms
  • Admitted: year the student was admitted (redudant, as this info is implicit in the ID, but has a uniform format here).
  • Polled: year in which the data was collected.

Submitted by Luis Acuña, professor of Mathematics, Instituto Tecnológico de Costa Rica. The data were collected by his Statistics students in various semesters as a field assignment. They are not trained pollers, so their samples may not be good representatives of the population
