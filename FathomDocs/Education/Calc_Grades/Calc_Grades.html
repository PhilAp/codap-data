h3>Introduction

Grades recorded during several years for a Calculus 2 course at the Instituto Tecnológico de Costa Rica, with one case per student. The first exam has a reputation for being much harder than the others. The topics for each exam are the same each semester.

Attributes:

  • Semester: Year and semester that the course was taught (there are two semesters in the school year, named first and second).
  • Sex: of the student
  • Exam1, Exam2, Exam3: grades on each of the major exams.
  • Course_grade: final grade, including the three exams plus quizzes, homework, etc.

Submitted by Luis Acuña, professor of Mathematics, Instituto Tecnológico de Costa
