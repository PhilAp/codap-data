Do students who take standardized tests multiple times have an advantage over those who just take them once or twice?

Each of cases in the collection represents a student taking a standardized test that has a mean score of 100 and a standard deviation of 20. Each student takes the test 6 times and each score is independent of the other 5 scores. (No learning!)

The attributes max2, max3, ..., max6 compute the maximum score out of 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6 tests.

So, in this model, how many points are gained on the average, by taking the test a second time? How does that compare with taking it the 6th time?
