Introduction

Grades obtained by Business students at Texas A and M University during the years 1989-1992. Each case is a student.

Attributes

  • Sex: of the student
  • Ethnicity: ethnic origin.
  • Grade: final grade in the course on a 0-100 scale
  • Z_score: (grade - (section's mean grade)) / (section's standard deviation).

Submitted by Luis Acuña, professor of Mathematics, Instituto Tecnológico de Costa Rica, who taught the sections shown here.
