Introduction

Grades obtained by students in the author's classes at the Instituto Tecnológico de Costa Rica. Each case is a student's grade in a course.

Attributes:

  • Year: year the course was taken.
  • Semester: semester within the year (there are two semesters in a school year, named first and second).
  • Course: name of the course.
  • Section: section number.
  • Office hours: number of visits during the semester to the professor's office for consultation.
  • Sex: of the student
  • Grade: final grade in the course, in the scale 0-100.

Submitted by Luis Acuña, professor of Mathematics, Instituto Tecnológico de Costa Rica.
