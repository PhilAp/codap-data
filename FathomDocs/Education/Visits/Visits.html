Introduction

Visits of students to author's office hours. (Author is a mathematics and statistics professor. See below.) Each case is a visit.

Attributes

  • Semester: year and semester number (there are two semesters in a year, named first and second).
  • Week: week number within the semester (semesters are 16 weeks long, but after those come final exams).
  • Day of week: 1=Monday, 2=Tuesday, etc.
  • Course: name of the course consulted.
  • Sex: sex of the student.

Submitted by Luis Acuña, professor of Mathematics, Instituto Tecnológico de Costa Rica, who has been recording each student's visit to his office hours for the last few years.
