Getting Started

Using a tide and current predictor, we gathered data of the tide patterns of Alcatraz Island, in California, and Birch Island, in Maine. For the Alcatraz Island data, we used the tides of July 1, 2005, to August 25, 2005. For the Birch Island data, we used the tides of April 1, 2005 to May 26, 2005.

Attribute Key:
  • date = the date of the recording
  • time = the time of the recording in 24-hr format
  • PDT = Pacific Daylight Time, the time zone of the recording
  • UTC = Universal Time Coordinated, the time zone of the recording
  • height = the height of the tide in feet
  • hourinday = the hour in the day when the recording was taken
  • dayNumber = the number of the day the recording was taken
  • decDays = the decimal day that the recording was taken

Source: All of this information was provided by Tim Erickson, December 2004
