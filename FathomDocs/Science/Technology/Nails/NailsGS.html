Getting Started

A penny is the size of a nail. This case table shows you the measurements associated with each penny. The unit of a penny is expressed with a d.

Attribute Key:
  • penny = size of nail
  • length = length of nail
  • diameter = diameter of nail
  • NperPound = the number of nails per pound (lb) per size
