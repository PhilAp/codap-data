Getting Started

Cars were selected at random from among 1993 passenger car models that were listed in both the Consumer Reports issue and the PACE Buying Guide. Pickup trucks and Sport/Utility vehicles were eliminated due to incomplete information in the Consumer Reports source. Duplicate models (e.g., Dodge Shadow and Plymouth Sundance) were listed once. The only missing values are for Cylinders in the rotary engine Mazda RX-7, and RearRoom for the two-seaters (Corvette and RX-7).

Further description can be found in the "Datasets and Stories" article "1993 New Car Data" in the Journal of Statistics Education (Lock 1993).

Sources:   Lock, R. H. (1993), "1993 New Car Data," Journal of Statistics Education, 1, No. 1.
Student Edition of Execustat (1990), Boston, MA: PWS-KENT Publishing Co.
