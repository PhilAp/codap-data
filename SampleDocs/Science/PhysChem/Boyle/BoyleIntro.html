Getting Started

This case table shows the data of Charles Boyle's Pressure vs. Volume Experiment in 1662.

Attribute Key
  • A_ShorterLeg - The number of equal spaces in the shorter leg, that contained the same parcal of air diversely extended.
  • B_HgHeight - The height of the mercurial cylinder in the longer leg, that compressed the air into those dimensions.
  • C_HgHtToBalanceAtmosphere - The height of the mercurial cylinder that counter-balanced the pressure of the atmosphere.
  • D_HgTotalHt - The Aggregate of the last columns B and C, exhibiting the pressure sustained by the included air.
  • E_PredictedHt - What that pressure should be according to the hypothesis, that supposes the pressures and expansions to be in reciprocal proportions.
