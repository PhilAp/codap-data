Height Over Time

Heights at different times of the day for several subjects (ages between 20 and 25). They had their heights measured at different times during the day. Each case is a measurement.

Attribute Key:
  • Subject = The number of the subject.
  • Sex = The sex of the subject.
  • Time_hrs = The time of day in the 24-hr format with decimals (ex. 8:45 PM is seen in the table as 20.75). An entry larger than 24.00 indicates a time after midnight, but before the subject went to bed.
  • Height_m: height, in meters.
Create your own graph:
  • Create a new graph by clicking on the Graph icon.
  • Drag a column header, an attribute, to the graph y-axis. You should see an orange rectangle in the different areas of the graph that you can add attributes to.
  • Drag another attribute to the x-axis. Is there a relationship between the x-axis attribute and the y-axis attribute?
  • To remove an attribute from an axis, click on the axis label and select "Remove X" or "Remove Y."
