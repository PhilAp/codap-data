Introduction

Attribute Key
  • Day 1-39 = Day of flight (Day 1=Apr 7;Day 29=May 5,2002)
  • Time 0-23 = Time of measurement (taken on the hour)
  • AirTemp_CH4 = Air temperature (degrees Celsius)- Chamber 4
  • Humidity_CH4 = Relative Humidity-Chamber 4
  • Light_CH4 = Light level - Chamber 4
  • CO2_CH4 = CO2 level - Chamber 4
  • CO2_Cabin = CO2 level - Cabin
  • Intake Temp = Air temperature (degrees Celsius - Cabin
  • Intake Humidity = Relative Humidity - Cabin
  • Case Number = 1-696 Observation number, in order

Studies like these are helping us learn how to grow plants in space. Among other things, plants can help scrub CO2 from the air and purify water.

Plant Chamber 4 contains Brassica plants that are growing aboard the ISS. You can see daily photos of the plants at www.orbitec.com/outreach/dailyimages.html.
