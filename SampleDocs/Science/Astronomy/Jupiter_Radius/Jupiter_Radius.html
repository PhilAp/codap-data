ATTRIBUTES
  • mdj: mean Julian date. It is measured in days from 17 November 1858.
  • radius: the apparent equatorial radius of Jupiter in arc seconds. One arc second is 1/60 of an arc minute, and an arc minute is 1/60 of a degree. So an arc second is small. There are 206, 265 of them in a radian.
