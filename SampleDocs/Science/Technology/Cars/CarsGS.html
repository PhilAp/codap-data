Getting Started

Cars were selected at random from among 1993 passenger car models that were listed in both the Consumer Reports issue and the PACE Buying Guide. Pickup trucks and Sport/Utility vehicles were eliminated due to incomplete information in the Consumer Reports source. Duplicate models (e.g., Dodge Shadow and Plymouth Sundance) were listed once. The only missing values are for Cylinders in the rotary engine Mazda RX-7, and RearRoom for the two-seaters (Corvette and RX-7).

Further description can be found in the "Datasets and Stories" article "1993 New Car Data" in the Journal of Statistics Education (Lock 1993).

Attribute Key:
  • Manufacturer = the manufacturer of the car
  • Model = the model of the car
  • MidPrice = the mid price of the model in thousands of dollars
  • CityMPG = the mi/gal attained while driving in the city
  • HghwayMPG = the mi/gal attained while driving on the highway
  • AirBagStandard = the placement of airbags if any
  • DriveTrainType = the type of drive train
  • NumCylinder = the number of cylinders in the engine
  • EngineSize = the size of the engine in litres (L)
  • HorsePower = the amount of horsepower the car has
  • ManualTrans = if the car has the capability to have manual transmission
  • TankCapacity = the capacity of the fuel tank in gallons
  • PassCapacity = the capacity of passengers in persons
  • Length = the length of the car in inches (in)
  • Width = the width of the car in inches (in)
  • RearRoom = the amount of room in the rear seat in inches (in)
  • Weight = the weight of the car in pounds (lb)
  • Domestic = whether or not the car was made in the US or not

Sources:   Lock, R. H. (1993), "1993 New Car Data," Journal of Statistics Education, 1, No. 1.
Student Edition of Execustat (1990), Boston, MA: PWS-KENT Publishing Co.
