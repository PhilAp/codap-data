Getting Started

This case table provides aircraft operating statistics from 1999. The figures shown are averages for most commonly used models.

Attribute Key:
  • Plane = the plane's model number
  • Seats = the number of seats on the plane
  • Cargo_Payload = the amount of cargo the plane can hold in pounds (lb)
  • Speed = the speed of the plane in knots (kt)
  • Flight_Length = the length of flights in nautical miles (N.M.)
  • Fuel = the amount of fuel it take to operate the plane in gallons per hour (gal/hr)
  • Operating_Cost = the amount of money it takes to operate the plane in dollars per hour (dollars/hr)

Source: http://www.atac.ca/About_The_Industry/Aircraft_Operating_Statistics.html
