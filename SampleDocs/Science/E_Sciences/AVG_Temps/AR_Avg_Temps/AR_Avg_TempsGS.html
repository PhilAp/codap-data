Getting Started

The United States Historical Climatology Network has stations to collect data about the weather. This is a collection of data from multiple stations around Arkansas over a period of about 100 years, ending in 1994.

Attribute Key:
  • StationNumber = the station's number
  • Year = the year the data was recorded
  • AvgAnnualTemp = the average annual temperature for the area around the station
  • Lat_N = the distance north from the equator
  • Long_W = the distance west from the Prime Meridian
  • Elevation = the elevation of the station
  • StationName = the name of the station
  • State = the state from which the data was taken

See http://cdiac.esd.ornl.gov/r3d/ushcn/ushcn.html for a full description.
