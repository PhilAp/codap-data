Getting Started

Attribute Key:
  • PST = Pacific Standard Time
  • Day = the day of the year
  • Year = the year of the recording
  • Mean_Temp = the mean temperature recorded
  • Mean_Dew = the mean dew point recorded in degrees Fahrenheit
  • Mean_Humidity = the mean humidity recorded in degrees Fahrenheit
  • Mean_Visibility = the mean amount of visibility recorded
  • Mean_Wind = the mean wind speed recorded in MPH
  • Max_Gust = the max gust speed recorded in MPH
  • Precipitation = the amount of precipitation recorded in inches
  • CloudCover = the amount of cloud cover recorded
  • Events = any weather events other than sun recorded
