Getting Started

Tasmania University in Hobart, Australia, recorded a seismograph of the Kobe earthquake on January 16, 1995. They started at 20:56:51 (GMT) and continued for 51 minutes at 1 second intervals.

Attribute Key
  • Time = the date and time of the recording
  • Vert_acc = the vertical acceleration in nm/s^2

Source: Data management centre, Washington University.
