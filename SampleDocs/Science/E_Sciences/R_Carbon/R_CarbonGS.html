Getting Started

The case table is a collection of regional CO2 emissions from the burnin gof fossil-fuels, manufacturing cement, and gas flaring from 1751-2009. All emission level estimates are expressed in thousand metric tons of carbon.

Attribute Key:
  • Region = the region the data was taken from
  • Year = the year the data was taken
  • Total = total levels of CO2 emission found in that region
  • Gas = levels of gas type CO2 emission found in that region
  • Liquid = levels of liquid type CO2 emission found in that region
  • Solid = levels of solid type CO2 emission found in that region
  • Cement = levels of cement type CO2 emission found in that region
  • Flaring levels of cement type CO2 emission found in that region
