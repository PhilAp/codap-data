Getting Started

Mr. E. O. Frye is a volunteer weather observer for the National Weather Service. Using their instruments and procedures, Mr. Frye gathered weather information about Marion, Iowa, for 1096 days. This information is shown in the case table.

Attribute Key:
  • Year = the year 19XX
  • Month = month of the year
  • Day = day of the month
  • DecimalDay = number of days since the first observation
  • MaxTemp = maximum temperature in degrees Fahrenheit
  • MinTemp = minimum temperature in degrees Fahrenheit
  • ObsTemp = temperature observed in degrees Fahrenheit
  • Precip = total precipitation in inches
  • Snowfall = total snow fall in inches
  • SnowOnGround = the amount of the snow on the ground in inches

This data is from 1983 - 1985. Data was supplied to the Nightingale Network by Chris Olsen.
