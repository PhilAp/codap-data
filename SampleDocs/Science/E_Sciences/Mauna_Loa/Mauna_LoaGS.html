Getting Started

From 1958 to 2010, the levels of CO2 in parts per million were recorded. Parts per million can also be equally measured 1:1 in milligrams per litre, so for ease of understanding in this activity, we will be expressing CO2 levels in milligrams per litre (mg/L).

Attribute Key:
  • year = year the data was taken
  • month = month the data was taken
  • year_dec = decimal year the data was taken
  • CO2 = amount of CO2 in mg/L
  • season_adj = CO2 levels adjusted for the season

Source: Data downloaded and adjusted by Tim in January, 2011 from ftp://ftp.cmdl.noaa.gov/ccg/co2/trends/co2_mm_mlo.txt
