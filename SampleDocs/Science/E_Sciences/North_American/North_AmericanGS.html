Getting Started

This as compilation of North American earthquakes from 1920 to 1989 of the magnitude 4.5 and higher.

Attribute Key:
  • year = year of the earthquake
  • month = month of the earthquake
  • day = day of the earthquake
  • lat = degree of latitude
  • long = degree of longitude
  • mag = the magnitude of the earthquake

Source: Extracted by Tim Erickson, July 1999.
Data from the National Earthquake Information Center, http://wwwneic.cr.usgs.gov/neis/epic/epic.html
