Source

Recalling a suggestion by Tim Erickson (www.eeps.com), that the election data available for download from the USA Today website could be used in an activity, two excellent websites were located: www-personal.umich.edu and www.princeton.edu. Michael Gastner, a Ph.D. Candidate in Physics at the University of Michigan provided the data files with the census data by county and the latitude and longitude of each county (available online at various locations, including arcdata.esri.com). Michael, together with Cosma Shalizi and Mark Newman, used election results downloaded from the USA Today site in the days following the election. There was significant work done at the University of Michigan to amalgamate the county data into one file. Each county is represented as a polygon and the latitude and longitude of each vertex was supplied. I only used one vertex's coordinates for each county in this file.

Other sources used in this activity:
