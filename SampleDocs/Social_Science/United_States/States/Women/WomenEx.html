Attribute Key
  • State = the state the data was taken from
  • Poverty = percent of women in poverty
  • OwnBusiness = percent of businesses that are woman-owned
  • CentsEarned = cents earned per male dollar for equal work
  • InLaborForce = percent of women in labor force (not percent of labor force that is women)
  • EmpldInManagerial = percent of women working in managerial or professional positions
  • 4yrCollege = percent of women with a 4-yr college degree
  • NoHealthIns = percent of women without health insurance
