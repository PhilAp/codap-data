Getting Started

The information in the case table shows the statistics of receipts from restaurants, caterers, and taverns in Alberta, Canada. The data is limited to purchases made by people 20-24 years old.

Attribute Key:
  • Year = year the data was taken from
  • Receipts = amount in thousands of dollars spent on drinking at a restaurant, tavern, or on a caterer
  • People = number of 20-24 year old people that spent that amount
