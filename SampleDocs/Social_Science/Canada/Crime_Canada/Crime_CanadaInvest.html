Create your own graph:
  • Create a new graph by clicking on the Graph icon.
  • Drag a column header, an attribute, to the graph y-axis. You should see an orange rectangle in the different areas of the graph that you can add attributes to.
  • Drag another attribute to the x-axis. Is there a relationship between the x-axis attribute and the y-axis attribute?
  • Remove an attribute on an axis by clicking on the axis label and selecting "Remove X" or "Remove Y." You can also replace it by dragging the new attribute on top of the old one.
  • Color the data in the graph by dragging an attribute into the orange rectangle in the center of it.
  • Play around with different attributes in the graph and see the relationships!
