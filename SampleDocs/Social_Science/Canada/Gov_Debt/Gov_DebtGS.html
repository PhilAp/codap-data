Getting Started

Here is information about the Canadian government's debt. This information for this case table was extracted from E-STAT Cansim Table 385-0010 on Statistics Canada's Internet Site, at http://www.statcan.ca/english/Estat/licence.htm on November 24, 2004.

Attribute Key:
  • Year = year the data was taken from
  • Fed_Debt_In_Millions = the amount of debt the government owes in millions of Canadian dollars
