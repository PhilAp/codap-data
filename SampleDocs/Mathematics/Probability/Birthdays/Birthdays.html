This data gives the distribution of births in the U.S. from 2000-2014, as provided by the Social Security Administration. The reporters on FiveThirtyEight posted the data on Github for an article.

Professor Geoffrey Berresford used data like this in his article, "The uniformity assumption in the birthday problem, Math. Mag. 53 1980, no. 5, 286-288."

If you plot a times series of the data you will have a nice example of periodic data.
