How can I remove bad data?

    From CODAP
  • Hide it in the graph. (See How do I look at only part of my data?)
  • Select the data and delete it from the table. Note: deleted data is gone forever—you cannot get it back!

    • From the sensor data collector
  • When you send data from the data collector, select only a portion. Or don’t export it at all – do a new run!
